About Me

I am an Arizona Native. I graduated from UofA and then went to work at Intel. I have had a great career and traveled all over the world for my job. My husband, my brother and my sister in-law all work at Intel as well!

I am currently a Senior Test Engineer and I have extensive experience in Program Management. I have developed and deployed many data systems for Intel test manufacturing giving me experience in database systems, automation for test equipment, unit traceability, writing training documentation and teaching.
